## Colors
- Primary: #0F6E56 (dark teal, HSL 160 75% 24%) — header, text links, filled elements
- Primary-light: #1D9E75 (HSL 155 68% 37%) — gradient pair for header/hero
- CTA: #EF9F27 (amber gold, HSL 37 87% 55%) — confirm/action buttons ONLY

## CTA Scarcity Rule
Max 1 gold CTA button per page. Scarcity drives attention to the real action.

## Rationale
- Teal = trust + modern, differentiated from PayPal/Wise
- Gold = Thai cultural resonance (temples, markets) + urgency/action psychology
- Success screen: gradient shift dark→bright teal as reward signal
- Amount display: ฿ largest on confirm page, foreign currency secondary
